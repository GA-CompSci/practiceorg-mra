Fem Club's

Projects & Initiatives

Take a look at some of our hands-on projects and awareness events that bring our mission to life through community impact and advocacy.

Feminine Hygiene Drive

Spring semester we collect and distribute essential hygiene products for women in shelters ensuring access to basic necessities with dignity.

Learn More

Christmas “Adopt a Family”

During the holidays, Fem Club come together to sponsor families in need, providing gifts, essentials, and a sense of care and community during the Christmas season.

View Story

Period Pain Simulator

This interactive project invites students and teachers to experience a period pain simulator, sparking conversations about empathy, stigma, and women’s health education.

Watch Demo

Denim Day & Sexual Assault Awareness Week

We organize events to raise awareness about sexual assault, and wear jeans in representation of sexual assualt awareness, to promote consent education, and support survivors through advocacy and solidarity.
